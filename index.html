ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Michael, a full stack developer living in Miami.

I specialize in building visually compelling, responsive, professional websites that are built with best practices. My expertise is in front-end development using HTML, CSS, Javascript, Wordpress and I also have a passion for design and back-end development. My projects are hand coded, and I enjoy bringing them to life from sketches and wireframes to a finished product.

I love to travel, take photos, and cycle in my free time and am always looking for exciting new projects to work on.
